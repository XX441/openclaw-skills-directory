Step back from the immediate context and examine the broader picture.

## Process

1. Restate the current situation in one paragraph.
2. Identify the larger systems/forces at play.
3. Map connections between this and other projects, pipelines, or strategies.
4. Surface assumptions that may be limiting thinking.
5. Question whether we're solving the right problem.

## Lens to Apply

- **Strategic** — How does this connect to IGSO's trust infrastructure narrative?
- **Pipeline** — Which BD pipelines or partners does this connect to?
- **Timeline** — Where does this fit in the 3-month, 6-month, 12-month view?
- **Resource** — What would it take to do this right? At scale?
- **Opportunity cost** — What are we NOT doing by doing this?

## Output

End with: "The bigger picture here is..." followed by 2-3 strategic insights.
